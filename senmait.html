Career Ronaldo is considered one of the greatest soccer players of all time. He's won five Ballon d'Or awards, the FIFA award for best player in the world. He's also the first men's player to score 900 career goals in official matches. His full name is Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro. His father named him after Ronald Reagan, his favorite movie actor and the U.S. president at the time of his birth. He has four children. Income Ronaldo's income comes from multimillion-dollar contracts, endorsements, and brand partnerships. He's been associated with major brands like Nike, Herbalife, and Clear.
